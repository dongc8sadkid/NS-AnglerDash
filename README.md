# NS-AnglerDash

请吃 https://nsmomola.nakki163.workers.dev/

A personal pre-trip dashboard built by a one-month fishing newbie who has yet to catch anything.
Born from the frustration of converting oz to g, kPa to inHg, and having seventeen tabs open before leaving the house. Pulls live weather, pressure trends, wind, and tide data for Nova Scotia; includes a unit converter, knot guide, and curated links to DFO and the NS Angler's Handbook — none of it reproduced, all of it pointed to.
Built with very limited HTML knowledge and an embarrassing amount of vibe coding. May or may not contain easter eggs. If you find them, please don't tell anyone. Not my fault. Blame Yoshi-P.
入坑1个月的钓鱼菜狗（仍未破龟）搓的个人行前仪表盘。
源于将盎司换算成克、千帕换算成英寸汞柱，以及出门前不得不打开十七个标签页的种种烦恼。该仪表盘实时获取NS的天气、气压趋势、风力和潮汐数据；包含单位换算器、鱼线扣指南（只放了一两个我能看懂的），以及精选的加拿大渔业与海洋部（DFO）和新斯科舍省《垂钓者手册》的链接——所有内容均未复制，仅提供指向。
仅凭极其有限的HTML知识和睿智Vibe Coding打造而成。
